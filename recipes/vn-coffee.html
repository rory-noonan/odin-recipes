Ingredients

  • 30 grams (2 tbsp) coarse ground dark roast coffee
  • 60 ml (4 tbsp) sweetened condensed milk
  • 16 oz glass filled with ice
  • kettle-boiled water

Steps

  1. Add sweetened condensed milk to a glass (not to be confused with the glass of ice.)
  2. Place phin (metal cup) on top of glass of sweetened condensed milk.
  3. Remove the inner filter from phin.
  4. Add coffee to phin. Slowly press the inner filter down on top of the coffee.
  5. Slowly add 50 ml of boiled water to phin.
  6. Once the coffee is done draining from phin, remove phin off the glass.
  7. Using a spoon, mix sweetened condensed milk with coffee.
  8. Pour coffee over glass of ice, or enjoy hot.
